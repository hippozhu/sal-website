Who are we

Shine A Light is a speech contest built for students who are ready to stand up, speak clearly, and share ideas that matter.

Established in 2020, the Shine a Light platform encourages young Chinese Americans to understand, care, and engage in community service and cultivating communication, leadership, and problem-solving skills.

We are a team of volunteers who are passionate about helping students grow and succeed.

What to expect

Each season brings together students, coaches, and judges around a shared celebration of voice and preparation. Participants receive clear expectations, feedback grounded in respect, and recognition for both courage and craft.

Browse Winners to see recent honorees, and use Register Today when sign-up opens for your group. If you coordinate the program for a school or organization, we are glad to help you get started through your usual contact channel.
